# Ch03 Q05: ReLU Positive Homogeneity

## Prompt

Show that multiplying a ReLU input by a positive constant is equivalent to
multiplying the ReLU output by that same constant.

## What This Tests

Whether the learner can reason from the piecewise definition of ReLU.

## Hints

- Split the proof into `z < 0` and `z >= 0`.
- The constant must be positive so it does not flip the sign of `z`.
