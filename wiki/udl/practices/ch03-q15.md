# Ch03 Q15: Maximum 3D Regions

## Prompt

Find the maximum possible number of 3D linear regions created by the network in
figure 3.11.

## What This Tests

Whether the learner can generalize region counting from lines in 2D to planes
in 3D.

## Hints

- Three planes in 3D can divide space into octant-like regions in the maximal case.
- Compare with figure 3.10c.

## Solution Notes

Selected answer available on booklet page 10.

## Self-Check Rubric

- The answer gives a maximum, not a guaranteed count.
- The geometric explanation matches three intersecting planes.
