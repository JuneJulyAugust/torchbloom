# Chapter 2 Reading Guide

## What This Chapter Teaches

Chapter 2 builds the core learning loop: define a model, compare predictions to
training targets with a loss, then adjust parameters to reduce that loss.

## Before You Read

Be comfortable with a line on a graph, a slope and intercept, and substituting a
number into a formula. For the problems, review derivatives as slope and partial
derivatives as "one parameter at a time" slopes.

## Section Map

- Page 31: supervised learning overview and function notation.
- Pages 32-33: linear regression model and least-squares loss.
- Pages 34-35: visualizing fit quality and the loss surface.
- Page 36: training, testing, underfitting, and overfitting.
- Pages 37-38: discriminative versus generative regression and problems.
