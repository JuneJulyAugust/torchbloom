# Chapter 1 Reading Guide

## What This Chapter Teaches

Chapter 1 is a tour, not a technical chapter. Treat it as a promise of what the
book will explain later: functions that map inputs to outputs, parameters that
control those functions, and training procedures that choose useful parameters.

## Before You Read

You only need the ordinary idea of a function: put something in, get something
out. If notation feels dense, start with the bridge page `functions-as-machines`.

## Section Map

- Pages 15-19: what deep learning is trying to do.
- Pages 20-26: what kinds of problems deep learning handles.
- Pages 27-30: how the book, problems, and notebooks should be used.

## Concepts To Master

- model as a function,
- input and output,
- parameter,
- training data,
- practice as part of reading.

## Equations To Understand

No core chapter equations are promoted yet. Use this chapter to prepare for the
notation in chapters 2 and 3.
