# Shallow Neural Networks

## Core Idea

A shallow neural network has one hidden layer. It computes hidden units from the
input, passes them through an activation function, and combines the activations
to make the output.

## Why It Matters In UDL

Chapter 3 shows how adding hidden units lets the model move beyond a single
line while preserving the supervised-learning loop from Chapter 2.

## High-School Bridge

Think of several simple line pieces being clipped, scaled, and added together.
The final curve can bend because different pieces turn on in different regions.
