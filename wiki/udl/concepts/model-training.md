# Model Training

## Core Idea

Training is the process of choosing parameters that make a model's predictions
fit data well according to a loss function.

## Why It Matters In UDL

Chapter 2 introduces the idea with a visible loss surface. Chapter 3 keeps the
same training idea but applies it to a more flexible function family.

## High-School Bridge

Imagine standing on a hill while blindfolded. If you can feel which way the
ground slopes upward, you can step the other way to go downhill.
