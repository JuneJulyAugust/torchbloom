# Universal Approximation

## Core Idea

With enough hidden units, a shallow network can approximate many continuous
functions as closely as desired on a bounded domain.

## Why It Matters In UDL

The theorem explains why a one-hidden-layer network is theoretically powerful.
It does not say the network is easy to train or efficient.

## High-School Bridge

Approximating a curve with many short line segments is already familiar. More
segments can trace a more complicated shape.
