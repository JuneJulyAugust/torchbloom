# Equation 2.4: 1D Linear Regression

## Equation

`y = f[x, phi] = phi_0 + phi_1 x`

## Symbols

- `x`: input value.
- `y`: predicted output.
- `phi_0`: intercept.
- `phi_1`: slope.

## Intuition

The equation describes a family of lines. Choosing `phi_0` and `phi_1` selects
one line from that family.
