Problem 17.5 The reparameterization trick computes the derivative of an expectation of a function f[x]:

$$
\frac{\partial}{\partial\phi}\mathbb{E}_{\Pr(x|\phi)}\left[\mathbf{f}[x]\right]
\qquad (17.33)
$$

with respect to the parameters $\phi$ of the distribution $\Pr(x|\phi)$ that the expectation is over. Show that this derivative can also be computed as:

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial}{\partial\phi}\mathbb{E}_{\Pr(x|\phi)}\left[\mathbf{f}[x]\right]&=\quad\mathbb{E}_{\Pr(x|\phi)}\left[\mathbf{f}[x]\frac{\partial}{\partial\phi}\log\left[\Pr(x|\phi)\right]\right]\\
&\approx\quad\frac{1}{I}\sum_{i=1}^{I}\mathbf{f}[x_{i}]\frac{\partial}{\partial\phi}\log\left[\Pr(x_{i}|\phi)\right].
\end{aligned}
\qquad (17.34)
$$

This method is known as the REINFORCE algorithm or score function estimator.
